Colorful City

I created "Colorful City" as an undergraduate student at Kennesaw State University (KSU) to learn how to use Adobe Illustrator while creating a lively image. I drew out the image from my imagination and used various tools in Adobe to create it. One of my intents with the project was to utilize color in a highly selective way and to let viewers see the world how I see it.
