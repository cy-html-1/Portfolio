A-frame Project

The A-frame project uses a 3D model created with the paid version of the Polycam app. The app uses technology similar to photogammetry to create 3D models from a 360 degree view around an object with various photos. The pumpkin model in view is a real pumpkin I bought and painted with an image of my dog as a Halloween decoration. I used my cellphone to capture the images and process them in Polycam. I chose to use the pumpkin to preserve it after it decomposes, as the image of my dog holds sentimental value. The project is a way to share the art and joy of my dog with others.
