Project 2

Catch Camellia

"Catch Camellia" is a project designed to be simple and engaging. When users hover over the image of Camellia, the white cat, an image of her appears with information below the image to indicate she has been caught. There is also a link to the American Veterinary Medical Association when she is caught to direct players to a webpage with information regarding spaying and neutering pets. I created the game from real life events. In my neighborhood, there is a woman who hoards cat who claims Camellia is her cat, although Camellia appears to be a community cat rather than one with an owner. Camellia arrived at my house in autumn 2023 to relax on the porch and in the yard, and in November of that same year, she was spotted at the side of the house with 4 kittens. I was helping Camellia raise her kittens, although the cat hoarder stole 2 of the cats. I have been trying to catch Camellia to spay her without success, although I did have the two kittens who were not stolen spayed and neutered after strategically catching them in humane traps. Camellia will not enter a human trap with food, thus it has been impossible to catch her. While Camellia is one unspayed community cat, the purpose of my game is to raise awareness of spaying and neutering animals, especially helping strays without resources, and to encourage people to have their pets spayed and neutered.
